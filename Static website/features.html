Features

Streamline your academic journey with our all-in-one Elevenrollment!

Sign Up / Sign In

This feature helps student to view their account summaries for the past and present semesters. This feature provides the account summary based on the list of courses students enrolled in a semester along with some extra charges for lab fee, library access fee, gym fee etc.

Registration

This feature allows students to add the course if they want to register to it. In case students change their mind, then they can drop that course too. This feature help students to make their enrollment process smooth.

E-forms

This feature helps students of our app to find all their important forms related to university under one roof. They can also request for some specific forms from their university authorities under this tab too.

Academic Progress

This feature allows students to keep track of information about their academic history, including GPA, transcripts, courses taken, academic achievements, and test scores.

Finances

This feature helps student to view their account summaries for the past and present semesters. This feature provides the account summary based on the list of courses students enrolled in a semester along with some extra charges for lab fee, library access fee, gym fee etc.

Calendar

This features helps the students to see the list of courses they took in the current semester. Also they can see the days, timings and location of each class they registered in. This will help our students to organise their classes so that they donâ€™t miss any upcoming class.

FAQs

Under this tab students can find list of all the frequently asked questions that students have while using our app.
